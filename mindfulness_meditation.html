Mindfulness Meditation

How to Perform

  1. Find a quiet and comfortable place to sit.
  2. Close your eyes and focus on your breath.
  3. Allow thoughts to come and go without judgment.
  4. Bring your attention back to your breath whenever your mind wanders.
  5. Continue for 10-15 minutes daily.

Dos and Don'ts

  • Do find a comfortable seated position.
  • Do not get frustrated if your mind wanders.
  • Do practice regularly for best results.
  • Do not practice in a noisy or distracting environment.

Benefits

  • Reduces stress and anxiety.
  • Improves concentration and focus.
  • Enhances emotional well-being.

Limitations

  • May be challenging for beginners.
  • Not a substitute for professional mental health treatment.
