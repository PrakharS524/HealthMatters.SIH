Kapalbhati Pranayam

Steps:

  • Sit in a comfortable position with your spine straight.
  • Take a deep breath in, and as you exhale, pull your stomach in towards your spine.
  • Repeat this cycle of inhalation and exhalation rapidly for 20 breaths.
  • Relax and breathe normally.

Dos and Don'ts:

Practice on an empty stomach, and avoid if you have high blood pressure or heart disease.

Benefits:

Improves respiratory efficiency, detoxifies the lungs, and aids in weight loss.

Limitations:

Not suitable for people with hernia or recent abdominal surgeries.
