Tadasana (Mountain Pose)

Steps:

  • Stand upright with your feet together.
  • Distribute your weight evenly on both feet.
  • Engage your thigh muscles and lift your kneecaps slightly.
  • Inhale, stretch your arms upward, and lengthen your spine.
  • Hold the pose for a few breaths, then release.

Dos and Don'ts:

Ensure that your spine is aligned and do not lock your knees.

Benefits:

Improves posture, strengthens legs, and increases height.

Limitations:

Those with low blood pressure should avoid holding this pose for long periods.
